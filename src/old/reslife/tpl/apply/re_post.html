관리자노트
The Residence Life Office will process the refund request. Residence hall charges, including damages will be deducted from the deposit. Any amount remaining from the deposit will be returned to the student in the form of a money order or via wire transfer. The money order will be mailed to the student’s mailing address, provided during the check-out procedure. Any address change requests should be directed to the Residence Life office via email before the deposit is refunded. The refund process is generally completed within 30 days. Requests for deposit refunds will be honored up to 6 months after the student has vacated residence. After completing and signing this form, a new residence hall application with the required deposit must be submitted to reapply for residence hall.
