INSPECTIONS
A resident move-in inspection will be conducted on the day of, or the day prior to, your lease commencement. This move in inspection will be used to note the condition of the residence before you take occupancy. A corresponding inspection will be performed when your lease expires and the apartment is vacated. This inspection provides a basis for determining what portion, if any, will be deducted from your security deposit as compensation for damages to the apartment.

PLEASE NOTE : A copy of the move-in inspection form will be left in you apartment for your review and completion. If you have any issues with regard to the condition of your apartment, you must make the appropriate notation on the inspection form, and return to the residence life office or to the security officer.
HOUSEKEEPING
Custodians are responsible for routine cleaning of public areas. No housekeeping services to apartments are provided for the IFH residents. For residents of CJ International House, short-term residents (1 month or less) will be provided with fresh towels twice per week and bathroom cleaning. It is recommended that you bring cleaning equipment in order to keep your room clean.
Your lease requires 30days written notice of your intent to vacate the premises at the end of your lease term. Please inform the residence manager in writing as soon as you know when you will be vacating. Please be advised that all residents are required to comply with their full lease term. Termination of a affiliation with Korea University does not automatically end your lease. There may be a substantial financial penalty for breaking a lease.
Procedure for vacating your apartment:
1. Send a letter or completed "Notice to Vacate" form to the residence life office. The letter must contain the exact date of your departure so a move-out inspection can be scheduled. Please keep in mind the original inspection performed upon your move-in is on file. You must also provide a forwarding address or Korean bank account information for the return of your security deposit.
2. Your residence must be left clean (including all appliances) and restored to its original conditions, excluding normal wear and tear.
3. Notify the post office of your new address to ensure that your mail is forwarded to you. Please note that the residence life office is not responsible for forwarding your mail.
4. Notify the residence life office of your new address or your Korean bank account in Korea so that your security deposit may be refunded to you. The residence life will refund your security deposit net of any offsets within 30days of your vacating your apartment. In the even to that you fail to give the residence life office your forwarding address, your deposit will be held until your notification. Be sure to return all keys to avoid key charges. Keys must be returned to the residence life office/security guard.

SECURITY DEPOSITS
Security deposits may not be used as payment for last month's rent. Security deposit accounts are maintained separately from rental accounts and cannot be transferred to cover rent payments prior to the time a tenant vacates. The security deposit is held to ensure that all lease items and conditions are complied with. To receive a full refund of the security deposit, the following requirements and procedures must be followed:
1. The full term of the lease has expired.
2. A full 30 days' written notice was given prior to vacating the residence, and there was no damage to the apartment beyond normal wear and tear.
3. The entire residence, including, but not limited to, the range, refrigerator, bathroom, closets, cabinets, cabinets, plumbing fixture, are clean and the refrigerator is defrosted.
4. There are no stickers, scratches, staples, holes or tape on any walls or doors.
5. There are no indentations, scratches, stains or burn marks in any flooring.
6. All rent has been paid. A forward address / a Korean domestic bank account have been given to the residence life office.
7. All keys have been returned.
8. All rubbish and debris has been removed from the unit and disposed of properly.
9. No tenant possessions are left in the apartment.
