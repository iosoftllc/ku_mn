Residence Hall Application Eligibility

All applying students must enroll for and earn a minimum of six (6) credit hours per semester, Except SUMMER and WINTER vacations.

Assignment Priority

All students should submit all required application materials online housing application by the application deadline.

Assignments are made based on the following sequence.

1. Foreigner freshman (newly enrolled first grader in regular undergraduate course)
2. New Exchange Students from the exchange agreement universities which has the terms of on-campus accommodation support.
3. New visiting students
4. International Graduate Students, including current exchange students, visiting students, foreigner freshman and transfer students.

IMPORTANT : Above are all on a priority date basis according to when all of the required application materials have been received.

As space is available, and in the following order:

5. Domestic Graduate Students on a priority date basis according to when all of the required application materials including the housing application deposit and online housing application have been received (for the CJ International House applications only).
6. Domestic Undergraduate Students on a priority date basis according to when all of the required application materials including the housing application deposit and online housing application have been received (for the CJ International House applications only).

* Registered students' housing is not guaranteed due to short supply, but every effort is made to accommodate you.
